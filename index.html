Dimmer

  • Wireless Dimming Receiver Module – External
  • Two 0-10V dimming control outputs
  • 12-36V Input
  • Compact form factor (33mm X 33mm)
  • Linear, Logarithmic, Exponential dimming options
  • 4 GPIOs, One I2C port, 3.3v@0.5A Aux Output
  • Solo(2), Dual, Dim-to-Warm, White Tunable (Programmable option)
  • Available in BT5.0, Thread/Zigbee options
  • Fully compliant Bluetooth mesh

Driver

  • Wide Input Range 12-40V
  • 98% Efficiency, Full range dimming
  • Dual LED Channel
  • BT/Thread Radio
  • Solo(2), Dual, Dim-to-Warm, White Tunable (Software option)
  • Linear, Logarithmic, Exponential dimming options
  • Expansion port
    • Aux 3.3V @0.5A
    • 4 PWM or GPIO
    • I2C
  • Available in 50W, 25W and 18W
  • Compact form factor (50mmX 60mm X 6mm)

Wall Switch

  • Fits into a standard wall socket
  • Solo(2), Dual, Dim-to-Warm, White Tunable (Software)
  • On/Off, Independent Up/Down for two dimming channel
  • Weekly scheduling backed up by battery
  • Available in BT, Thread/Zigbee options

    • Microphone (Security)
    • Cloud monitoring and control
  • Optional BT/Thread/
  • Zigbee Radio
